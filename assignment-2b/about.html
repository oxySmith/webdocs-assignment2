About Cottbus Delivery

We are a small but fast growing physical and online fresh food market located in Cottbus that offers fresh food delivery for you. Our company was established on 1st November,

2021 and has been running our services ever since. We offer local fresh food such as vegetables, meat, fish, bread and also drinks.

Our mission is to make our customers happy!

We work passionately to supply you with local fresh food and to save your time and effort by offering online order services so you can get your fresh food in your preferred time slot at your door or at our store.

We are a young company and are open to ideas. If you have any kind of ideas you want to share with us - please use the contact form and share your feedback to help us become better :)
